Framing App allows you to print large images by cutting them into small pieces printable onto standard office equipment (A5, A4 or A3 for example).

But whereas most solution only offer a regular tiling solution, Framing App asks you to chose how you want to map your image with the paper sheets and allows you to do so freely. You then just have to assemble the pieces together.

Do not cut out the white borders though! Think of them as frames through which your picture will reveal itself. Set up the frame borders to be as thick or tiny as you want (check what your printer can do first) and use them to hightlight the most important parts of your picture!

Still unsure of what it means? Check out what we did with The Scream for our logo.
